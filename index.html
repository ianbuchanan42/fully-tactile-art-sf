Fully Tactile Art SF

Why Tactile Art?

This project is a response to the continuous trend of art being enjoyed through vision, instead of also being seen through touch and other senses. We look to expand on this idea through creating a space for artists and patrons to explore this important conversation of what it means to "see" art.
